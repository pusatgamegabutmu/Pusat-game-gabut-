DOMINO CLOTHES PIN

A fun twist on classic dominoes! Lose? Get clipped with clothes pins!

How to Play

  • Match numbers on dominoes to build a chain
  • Be the first to play all your dominoes to win!
  • Loser gets clothes pins equal to their remaining dots
  • DOUBLE BLANKS? That's 10 extra clothes pins!
  • Tap a domino in your hand to select it, then tap PLAY
